Blanca Rosario

founder of Where's Eve


I'm a New York City native, daughter of immigrant parents. I have two beautiful children. One is an adult while I'm still raising one more. I have been married and divorced, still learning who I am but have continued on with my life goals just was a little detoured. I have lots to share and want to provide resources to other women like me. I don't believe I had the opportunity to experience full support from my parents or family and because of it I want to be the support another girl or woman needs.
